2. EDITING STRATEGY
The Hook: For every clip, identify the most controversial, funny, or shocking 3-5 seconds. Place this segment FIRST in the segments list, even if it happens in the middle of the original video.
Flow: Arrange subsequent segments to provide context to the hook. You are allowed to re-order the video (e.g., Start at 05:00, then jump back to 01:00) to keep the viewer watching.
Length: Each final clip should be between 10 to 25 seconds for maximum retention.

3. VISUAL & FRAMING LOGIC
Check the RESOLUTION in the report:
If Horizontal (e.g., 1920x1080): Use the Stacked Streamer Layout. This puts the Facecam on top and the screen content on the bottom.
Code: "frame_filter": "split[t][b];[t]crop=iw/3:ih/3:iw/1.5:0,scale=1080:960[face];[b]crop=iw:ih/2:0:ih/2,scale=1080:960[screen];[face][screen]vstack"
If Vertical or Single Person: Use the Standard Vertical Crop.
Code: "frame_filter": "crop=w=ih*9/16:h=ih:x=(iw-ow)/2"

Note: "When generating the frame_filter, always use the explicit multiplication symbol *.
For Standard Vertical: crop=w=ih*9/16:h=ih:x=(iw-ow)/2
For Stacked Stream: split[t][b];[t]crop=iw:ih/2:0:0,scale=1080:960[face];[b]crop=iw:ih/2:0:ih/2,scale=1080:960[screen];[face][screen]vstack
Ensure that for Captions, the end time of one caption is exactly the start time of the next to keep the horizontal white bar solid."

4. CAPTION SYSTEM (BLACK ON WHITE BAR)

Style: Short, punchy sentences (3-5 words max). Use emojis and try for call to action for like or subscribe but keep this natural and not force audiance and in starting dont try for call to action.
